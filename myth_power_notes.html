Section 01 of 07
Myth vs Reality
Myth is not falsehood. It is a functional construct.
Definition — Three Layers
  • Primitive layer — symbolic stories explaining existence
  • Social layer — shared narratives binding groups
  • Political layer — engineered belief systems to align behavior
Operational Properties
  • Compresses complexity into symbols
  • Creates meaning where none is inherent
  • Converts randomness into destiny
Mechanism
ElementCharacter
RealityFragmented, indifferent, complex
MythSimplified, coherent, satisfying
Human defaultCognitive ease over accuracy
Control over myth = control over interpretation of reality, moral framework, and collective action.
Critical implication — Section 01
Section 02 of 07
Nature of Fascism
Fascism is not ideology-first. It is power-first. Ideology is packaging.
Core Components
  • Ultra-nationalism — nation elevated above individual; identity becomes collective
  • Totalitarian structure — no separation between state, society, individual
  • Myth engine — glorious past, existential threat, destiny-driven future
  • Emotional primacy — logic suppressed; fear, pride, anger amplified
Language Engineering
"Believe"→suspend doubt
"Obey"→remove autonomy
"Fight"→channel aggression
Mechanism:repetition → internalization → belief

Hegel's Influence
History has a direction. Spirit evolves through conflict.
Key Constructs
  • Spirit (Geist) — collective consciousness; evolves through conflict
  • Dialectic — Thesis → Antithesis → Synthesis; conflict produces progress
  • State as highest form — individual finds meaning within state
Critical Distortion

Fascist adaptation:
State demands submission as highest duty
