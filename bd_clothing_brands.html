03 — Current Favorites
The two brands that never disappoint
Current top pick
Artisan
Cannot leave empty-handed
The current number one. What's unusual about Artisan isn't a specific product — it's that window shopping rarely stays as window shopping. The pull into an actual purchase is consistent and unexplained.
"Even window shopping often turns into a purchase. Hard to leave without buying something."
Reliable fallback
Infinity
Always find something
The reliable fallback. When nothing else seems right, Infinity delivers. It doesn't dazzle — it just reliably has something worth buying. That consistency is its own form of value.
"When nothing else works, Infinity always has something likable. Never a wasted trip."
