4 of 5 — Current favorites & future intent
Artisan · Infinity · STC · Tanzim's Preference hierarchy
Artisan — Current top pick
Window shopping almost always converts to a purchase. Hard to leave empty-handed. Strongest pull of any brand right now.
Infinity — Reliable fallback
When nothing else works, Infinity delivers. Always find something likable. Never a wasted trip.
STC — Future interest
Perceived as very posh / elegant. Not purchased yet — feels premium. Intent to buy exists.
Tanzim's — Future interest
Sub-brand of STC. Same posh perception — aspirational tier, not yet purchased.
Pattern: Current preference flows toward brands with strong in-store pull (Artisan) or guaranteed satisfaction (Infinity). Premium/posh brands (STC, Tanzim's) are admired but the price-quality hesitation creates a future-buy rather than now-buy dynamic.
